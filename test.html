Audiometry Test

Ensure you are in a quiet environment and wearing your Sennheiser PXC 550 II.

Strict Calibration Required: To map theoretical absolute Hearing Levels, you MUST set your OS master volume to 100%, and ensure the touch volume on the headphones is maximum.

Safety Note: A software safety lock is engaged. The test will not exceed 80 dB HL. It starts quietly at 30 dB HL and adapts to your hearing.

Validity Check: Hidden silent catch-trials are mixed into the test. An honest listener almost never responds to silence, so the number of responses during these silent windows is run through a binomial test to flag when a result looks like random guessing rather than genuine hearing. Press the button only when you truly hear a tone.
